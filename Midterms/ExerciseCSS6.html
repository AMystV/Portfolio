Introduction

Respawn is still a relatively new studio, but its legacy can be found in the modern first-person shooter in all its forms - from Medal of Honor to Modern Warfare and then, finally, to Titanfall. There's a through line there that's clear to see when you embark on Titanfall 2's brief but sumptuous campaign.

Campaign

The single-player campaign alone is good enough to justify the purchase price. Every mission has some twist and clever design element that keeps the campaign diverse and fresh. Much of it is so good that I'm tempted to jump right back in and play it all again.

Multiplayer Modes

  • Bounty Hunt: Kill enemy Pilots and AI, then deposit rewards into a bank.
  • Amped Hardpoint: Teams battle to control points for extra points.
  • Pilots vs. Pilots: Fight without Titans.
  • Capture the Flag: Classic flag-running mode.
  • Free For All: Everyone for themselves.
  • Last Titan Standing: Titans only, no Pilots allowed.
